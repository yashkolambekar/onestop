Youtube Channels



CodeWithHarry

CodeWithHarry is so far the biggest Indian channel on Youtube and it covers a range of programming languages and also topics related to programmers and developers, if you are not familiar with him, he is one of the most respected guys in the programming and development niche of Indian Youtube. If you are trying or planning to learn a programming language CodeWithHarry is someone I will suggest having a look at this channel

FreeCodeCamp

FreeCodeCamp (the website) is considered as an ultimate Bible for learning web development by a majority of devs out there, FCC also have a youtube channel where you will be able to find videos that go along with the curicullum/courses on their website, also this channel consists of a lot of quality video courses

Coding Ninjas

Coding Ninjas is a educational company and they also have a youtube channel where they talk about a wide variety of topics related to programming and development, you will also find them advertising their courses but you have to be smart enough to get information that you need and to ignore the rest

Online Tutorials

You are a web developer who designs their websites on own and likes cool animations and elements in the site, if this describes you Online Tutorials is a paradise for you as it teaches cool css tricks and tutorials that you cannot imagine of
