OneStop

We will be covering many important websites in this section, make sure you read the description provided with each site



W3Schools

Whenever you search for a term regarding the 3 languages we use, the first side that pops up is this, maybe the SEO is well optimised or is this the ultimate reference book?
It's true to an extent, this site consists of everything you need to know about development, though I don't suggest this site to learn web development but you can have a look, once in a while to keep refreshing your knowledge, also you will come to this site very often through google search

freeCodeCamp

The first site that I felt like has a well structured curicullum and this one is a bit different from the w3schools this site doesn't cover everything about a language but it gives a sense of how to use it in real life
I suggest using this site to learn web development in a structural and a progressing pattern but as soon as you get to know about a new term, learn it using w3schools, it will get lengthy but it will be worth it

Dev Community

This is the website you were looking for, a place where people can share their creations, information and many other useful stuff and a plus point - It's only for developers
From podcasts to tutorials and guidance, everything at one place and you can also chat to folks but you need to follow each other and that's not an hard thing. I feel this is a Facebook for Developers

CSS Tricks

Pretty self explanatory, CSS and Tricks
This one is all about Design, CSS and Amazing articles regarding it, you can find supercool stuff here and if you spend more time in this site, your design game will improve for sure

Reddit/r/webdev

Reddit can be the best platform to connect to people of same intrests and be a part of a community of smart people
Also make sure you don't share much of your personal information here, it's managed by the satan, shhhh!

Codepen

To give a short explanation, people share their creations here, you can get inspiration from these projects and also when you have to host small pieces of code to share or test this can be a good friend to your help

Hashnode

A community of devs where they share their experiences gained over all the years of hustle and struggle, tutorials and more
Also you will get your seperate blog, also the UI of this blog is simply great

CodeNewbie

Again words of widsom and a community of passionate developers and this one comes with a advantage that we don't have to sign up to use this site, make sure you check this one
